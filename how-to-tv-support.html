Quick answers

How do I mark episodes as watched?
Open a show and tick off episodes season by season. How To TV always keeps track of the next one for you.
Will it tell me when a show comes back?
Yes. You can get a notification when a returning series drops its next episode, plus an optional weekly digest.
Does my progress sync across devices?
Your watch progress and ratings sync privately through your own iCloud, so they're on all your devices and nowhere else.
Where does the show data come from?
Show, season, and episode details are provided by TMDb. Your personal watch history stays on your device and in your iCloud.
