What it does

A model of your taste, built only from your ratings.

Predicts your rating

A personal model, trained on the films you've rated, estimates how you'll score something before you watch it.

Modern and classic brains

Separate models for recent films and 15+ year-old classics, so older movies are judged on their own terms.

How you'll feel

Every film gets a clear Ugh, Good, or Great verdict, calibrated to your own history rather than a generic average.

Follows your taste

Recent favourites count for more, so predictions keep pace with how your taste actually shifts over time.

AI Discovery

Optional AI recommendations read your taste profile and surface films worth your night, on only when you enable it.

Private by design

Your ratings and any AI keys stay on your device. No ads, no analytics SDKs, no tracking.

Why it's different

It doesn't tell you what critics thought. It tells you what you'll think.

Privacy

Your ratings stay yours.

How To Movie is built around your own data. Your ratings, watchlist, and taste model live on your device, powered by film data from TMDb. AI Discovery is optional, runs only when you turn it on, and uses an API key you store yourself. No ads, no analytics, no tracking.
