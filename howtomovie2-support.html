Quick answers

How does the rating prediction work?
How To Movie builds a personal model from the films you've rated and uses it to estimate how you'll score a new film. The more you rate, the sharper it gets.
Why is a prediction different from the public score?
It isn't based on the crowd. The prediction reflects your taste and history, so it can differ a lot from an average rating.
Do I need the AI features?
No. AI Discovery is optional. It only runs if you turn it on and add your own API key, which is stored on your device.
Where is my data stored?
Your ratings, watchlist, and taste model are kept on your device. Film details come from TMDb.
