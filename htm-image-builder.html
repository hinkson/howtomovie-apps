What it does

Your movie taste, on your home screen.

Built from your library

Pull posters straight from the films you've rated and watchlisted on TMDb, with no hunting for artwork.

Four layout styles

Choose a poster grid, a fanned mosaic, a single hero band, or a layered collage for every wallpaper.

Full device resolution

Every image renders at your iPhone or iPad's exact resolution, so it's crisp the moment you set it.

Source and filter

Draw from your ratings, watchlist, a genre, or a studio, then filter by rating to get exactly the mix you want.

Save reusable jobs

Save a setup once and rerun it any time, or batch several jobs together in a single combined run.

Straight to Photos

Finished wallpapers save into a Photos album you pick, ready to set from your camera roll.

The feeling

A home screen that's actually yours.

Privacy

Your library stays yours.

Movie Canvas works from your own TMDb account and stores your sign-in and preferences on your device, with no ads, analytics, or tracking. Posters and film data come from TMDb, and generated wallpapers are only ever saved to Photos when you ask.
