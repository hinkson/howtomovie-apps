What it does

Everything you're watching, finally in one place.

Line up the shows you mean to start and keep them all in one tidy place.

New-episode alerts

Privacy

Your watch history stays yours.

How To TV is built local-first, with private iCloud sync and no third-party accounts, ads, analytics, or tracking. Your shows, progress, and ratings are stored on your device and in your own iCloud, powered by TV data from TMDb.
