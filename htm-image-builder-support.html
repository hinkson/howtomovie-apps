Quick answers

How do I make a wallpaper?
Sign in with TMDb, pick a source — your ratings, watchlist, a genre, or a studio — choose a layout, and tap Generate. Images render at your device's resolution.
Where do the posters come from?
Posters and film data are provided by TMDb, based on the movies in your rated list and watchlist.
Where are the wallpapers saved?
Generated images save into a Photos album you choose, so you can set any of them as your wallpaper straight from Photos.
Do I need a TMDb account?
Yes. You sign in with TMDb so the app can read your rated movies and watchlist. Your sign-in stays on your device.
What are jobs and combined runs?
A job saves a full setup — source, filters, layout, and album — so you can rerun it in one tap. A combined run chains several jobs together and generates them in order.
