What it does

Your movie wishlist, on price watch.

Buy & rent tracking

Deal Hunter follows the Apple TV purchase and rental price of every movie you're watching — regular price, current price, and the lowest it's ever seen.

A Wanted list

Search the full TMDb catalog, add a film to your Wanted list, and Deal Hunter starts watching its price from that moment on.

Price-drop alerts

Set your price and get a notification the moment a movie goes on sale — to buy or to rent. No more checking by hand.

Checks itself

Prices refresh quietly in the background, including a sweep for Apple's Tuesday new-release deals, so the app is up to date when you open it.

Price history

Every movie has a price chart and a rent-versus-buy view, so you can see whether today's price is actually a deal.

A library that remembers

Mark movies as owned and back your purchases up to a private list in your own TMDb account, so your library survives a new phone or reinstall.

The feeling

Less "is it on sale yet?" More "it's on sale."

Privacy

Your wishlist stays yours.

Deal Hunter is built local-first, with no ads, analytics, or tracking. Your Wanted list, library, and price history live on your device, powered by movie data from TMDb and public price lookups from Apple's iTunes Search API.
